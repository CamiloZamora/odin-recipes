Caesar Salad

Description:

The traditional Caesar salad contains some pretty basic ingredients. Normally, it's romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, croutons, and Caesar dressing. This iconic dressing is made from lemon juice, egg, garlic, Parmesan cheese, Worcestershire sauce, and anchovies. Even though the tossed salad only has four ingredients, it packs a major flavor punch.

Ingridients:

  • Chicken.
  • Salt.
  • Lemon jucie.
  • Mayonnaise.
  • Olive oil.
  • Cheese.
  • Black pepper.
  • Lettuce.
  • Pineapple.
  • Roasted bread.

Steps:

  1. Cook chicken.
  2. Cut chicken in cubes.
  3. Cut cheese in cubes.
  4. Wash and cut lettuce.
  5. Cut pineapple in cubes and reserve some jucie.
  6. Make dressing by mixing pineapple juice, mayonnaise, lemon juice, olive oil.
  7. In a bowl mix the chicken, cheese, roasted bread, pineapple and lettuce.
  8. Add to the bowl the mixture the dresing and mix carefully.
  9. Finally seasoning with salt and black pepper.
  10. Serve and enjoy!.
